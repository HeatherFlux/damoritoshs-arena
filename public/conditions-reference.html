PF2e / SF2e Conditions Reference

Quick reference for Damoritosh's Arena. Verified against Archives of Nethys (Remaster + SF2e).

Combat Conditions

Blinded Cannot see; -4 Perception
-4 status to Perception (if vision is only precise sense). Cannot see. Auto crit-fail vision-based Perception checks. Immune to visual effects.
Clumsy valued Penalty to Dex checks, AC, Reflex
Status penalty = value to Dex-based checks and DCs: AC, Reflex saves, ranged attack rolls, Acrobatics, Stealth, Thievery.
Concealed DC 5 flat check to target
Attacker must succeed at a DC 5 flat check when targeting you. Area effects bypass this.
Confused Off-guard; attack randomly
Off-guard. Attack wildly at random targets. Can't Delay, Ready, or use reactions. DC 11 flat check to recover when taking damage.
Controlled Controller dictates actions
No direct numeric penalties. Controller dictates all actions.
Dazzled Everything concealed
If vision is only precise sense, all creatures/objects are concealed from you (DC 5 flat check to target).
Deafened Cannot hear; -2 Perception
-2 status to Perception for initiative and sound-based checks. Cannot hear. DC 5 flat check for auditory actions. Immune to auditory effects.
Doomed valued Closer to death
Dying value at which you die reduced by doomed value. If max dying reaches 0, instant death. Decreases by 1 per full rest.
Drained valued Penalty to Con; reduced max HP
Status penalty = value to Con-based checks (including Fortitude). Lose level × value HP and reduce max HP by same. Decreases by 1 per full rest.
Dying valued Near death; recovery checks
Unconscious (grants -4 AC/Perc/Reflex, off-guard, cannot act, blinded). Recovery check at start of turn. Dying 4 = death. +1 on damage, +2 on enemy crit or crit-fail save.
Encumbered Clumsy 1; -10 ft Speed
Equivalent to Clumsy 1 (-1 AC, -1 Reflex, -1 Dex checks). -10 ft to all Speeds (min 5 ft).
Enfeebled valued Penalty to Str checks and damage
Status penalty = value to Str-based checks (melee attack rolls, Athletics) and Str-based damage rolls.
Fascinated -2 Perception and skills
-2 status to Perception and skill checks only (NOT attacks or saves). Can't use unrelated concentrate actions. Ends on hostile action against you/allies.
Fatigued -1 AC and saves
-1 status to AC and all saving throws. Can't use exploration activities. Recover after full rest.
Fleeing Must flee
Must spend all actions escaping. Can't Delay or Ready.
Frightened valued -1/turn Penalty to ALL checks and DCs
Status penalty = value to ALL checks and DCs (AC, attacks, saves, Perception, skills, spell DCs). Decreases by 1 at end of your turn.
Glitching valued Equipment malfunction (SF2e)
Flat check DC 5+value at turn start. On failure: item penalty = value to all checks/DCs. On crit fail: also stunned 1. On crit success: reduce value by 1.
Grabbed Off-guard; immobilized
Off-guard. Immobilized. DC 5 flat check for manipulate actions.
Off-Guard -2 AC
-2 circumstance to AC. Can apply to specific creatures or all.
Paralyzed Cannot act; off-guard
Off-guard. Cannot act (except Recall Knowledge and mind-only actions). Senses still function in visible area.
Persistent Damage valued Ongoing damage each turn
Take damage at end of each turn, then DC 15 flat check to recover. Assisted recovery reduces DC to 10.
Prone Off-guard; -2 attacks
Off-guard. -2 circumstance to attack rolls. Can only Crawl or Stand. Can Take Cover for +4 vs ranged.
Quickened +1 action per turn
Gain 1 additional action at start of turn. Often restricted to specific action types.
Restrained Off-guard; immobilized; limited actions
Off-guard. Immobilized. Can only attempt to Escape or Force Open bonds. Overrides grabbed.
Sickened valued Penalty to all checks; cannot ingest
Status penalty = value to ALL checks and DCs. Can't ingest anything. Can retch (1 action) to attempt Fort save to reduce by 1 (2 on crit success).
Slowed valued Fewer actions per turn
Reduce actions regained at start of turn by slowed value.
Stunned valued Cannot act; lose actions
Cannot act. Lose total actions equal to stunned value (may span multiple turns). Overrides slowed.
Stupefied valued Penalty to mental checks; spell disruption
Status penalty = value to Int/Wis/Cha-based checks and DCs (Will saves, spell attacks/DCs, Perception, mental skills). Spell disruption flat check DC 5+value.
Suppressed -1 attacks; -10 ft Speed (SF2e)
-1 circumstance to attack rolls. -10 ft status penalty to all Speeds.
Unconscious -4 AC/Perception/Reflex; cannot act
-4 status to AC, Perception, and Reflex. Off-guard. Cannot act. Cannot see. Fall prone, drop items.
Wounded valued Increases dying severity
When you gain dying, increase dying value by wounded value. Ends on Treat Wounds success or full HP + 10 min rest.

SF2e-Specific Conditions

Glitching valued Equipment malfunction
See above in combat conditions.
Suppressed Suppressive fire
See above in combat conditions.
Untethered Zero-g drift; cannot move normally
Off-guard. Can't take move actions. Drift 5 ft in last direction at end of turn. Typically also Clumsy 1. Use Push Off to change direction.

Custom Penalties

Status Penalty valued Generic status penalty
For spells/abilities that impose a status penalty not matching a named condition. Value applies to all checks and DCs. Use combatant notes to record source and scope.
Circumstance Penalty valued Generic circumstance penalty
For terrain, positioning, or situational circumstance penalties. Value applies to all checks and DCs. Use combatant notes to record source and scope.
